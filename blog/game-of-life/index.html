Game of Life

2020-09-25

When saying life is like a game, one might think it is like a choose-your-own-story RPG with different endings.
I like to think life is more like an open-world sandbox with lots of miscellaneous achievements.

When you play a game, it feels rewarding to take a look at your achievements so far every once in a while.
And so it does with life too.
That's why I think it can be beneficial to pause life now and then and think about your recent achievements.
For example, in the last six months, I have:

  • finished my degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science,
  • obtained my driver's license,
  • read all of Team Fortress comics,
  • watched all the Saturday Night Live digital shorts (at least all those I could find online),
